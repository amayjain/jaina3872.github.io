Introduction

Blackholes appear frequently in astronomy classes,
but they are never taught in depth. A general definition
is given most of the time, but on this webpage, more
information will be provided on these objects in space that are
relatively unknown.

What exactly are black holes and how do they affect us?

The general definition of a blackhole is an area
in space where the gravity is so strong that no matter can escape.

What will be found on the upcoming pages?

Page 1 will discuss the discovery of blackholes. Page 2 will discuss how
black holes interact with other space objects. Page 3 wil inform about proofs
of black hole existence and debates against it. Page 4 will discuss
on how black holes affect us.
